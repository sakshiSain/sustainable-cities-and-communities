7 Ways to make Every Day--:Earth Day

Go Outside More!

Stepping outside occasionally can help you appreciate nature in all its glory.

Use Reusable Products

Cut out the plastic and use reusable bags. Use natural material, sustainably made, reusable household needs when possible.

Use Transportation Mindfully

Take a walk to nearby places instead of using vehicles. Use if more and more public transportation instead of oersonal vehicles. This helps in multiple ways as it not minimizes pollution but also wastage of resources and energy.

Eat Plant-Based Meals

When you buy organic, you are contributing to a positive approach toward agriculture without harmful pesticides. Increase consumption of whole, local, organic, sustainably raised food.

Protect and Preserve Water

Fresh Water is a limited resource, making water conservation an important factor for the environment.Use water conciously without wasting it, reuse whenever possible and never pollute.

Plant more and more Trees!

Trees promote health and social well-being by removing air pollution, prevention of soil erosion, maintaing a balanced water cycle, a habitat to wildlife, maintaining eclogiacl balance, maintaining temperature, fulfilling the requirements of food, medicines, wood etc.

Awareness for saving Environment

Raising awareness of environmental issues is key to keeping sustainable living at the forefront of people's minds, but also an oppurtunity to educate people and spread the word to help protect nature.Socializing with family, freinds and society personally and virtually about the need to save environment.
